"Pepe's Salsarific Quest"

In a magical world where the balance between taste and chaos is at stake, an ancient curse has been cast. A colossal, malevolent chicken named "Gallus Rex," known as the "King of Chickens," has plunged the land into disorder. Its dreadful crow has distorted the flavors of the world, and only Pepe, the brave Salsamagician, can put an end to this menace.
